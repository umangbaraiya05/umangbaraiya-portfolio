A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Designer by training, builder by instinct,
curious by default.

// origins
Born in Ahmedabad, raised on Doordarshan title sequences and dial-up modems. ✦
// craft
I care about edge cases — the empty state, the error toast, the "what if they paste 4MB?" moments.
// approach
Always in "let me try this" mode. Build the rough thing, then sharpen it.
// experience
8 years across SaaS, fintech, AI tooling, and a few brave consumer apps. Shipped real things to real users.
// stack
